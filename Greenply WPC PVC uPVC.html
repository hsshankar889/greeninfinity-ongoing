Frequently Asked Questions

Need Answers? We got them

What are the benefits of a PVC board?

They can be used for structural purposes and as a decorative finishing material for surfaces. They have a smooth finished surface. The PVC board is homogenous & the density is equal at all points of the board.

Can we use PVC boards in kitchen?

PVC boards are ideal for kitchen. As PVC foam boards are fire-resistant. It can resist acid, moth, heat, vibrations, noise and are light-weight.

Are PVC boards durable?

Due to the structure of its component molecules, PVC foam boards are highly strong which ensures that they don’t undergo any deformation.

Is PVC suitable for outdoor applications?

Yes, PVC is durable and weather-resistant, making it an excellent choice for outdoor use.

How does PVC compare to traditional wood products?

PVC is low-maintenance, and resistant to moisture and pests, offering advantages over traditional wood.

How can PVC be painted to match different colour schemes?

Yes, PVC can be painted with compatible paints to achieve the desired colour or finish.
