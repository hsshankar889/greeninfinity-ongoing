About GreenInfinity

Driven by innovation and a commitment to excellence, GreenInfinity is a leading name in the wood panel industry, crafting sustainable and high-quality solutions for modern living.

Progressing Sustainably

At GreenInfinity, sustainability isn't just a buzzword; it's a core philosophy embedded in every aspect of our operations. We are dedicated to responsible resource management, minimizing our environmental footprint, and contributing positively to the communities we serve.

From sourcing raw materials from sustainably managed forests to implementing eco-friendly manufacturing processes and promoting circular economy principles, we strive to create products that are not only beautiful and durable but also kind to the planet. Our commitment extends to reducing waste, conserving energy, and investing in green technologies.

We believe that a thriving business and a healthy planet go hand in hand, and we are continuously exploring new ways to innovate for a more sustainable future.

GreenInfinity Journey

1984

Mr. Rajesh Mittal starts operations with a saw mill

1988

Setting up 1st plywood unit in Tizit, Nagaland

1990

Incorporated as company under the name Mittal Laminates Pvt. Ltd

1994

Transformed into public limited company - Mittal Laminated Limited

1996

Name changed Greenply Industries Limited

1997

Launch of Greenply brand (Flagship Premium Brand)

2005

Amalgamation of worthy plywood, now Kriparamour factory

2007

Acquisition of two plywood units in Gujarat, now in Bamanbore Factory

2008

Implementation of SAP and launch of Optima Brand & flush doors

2014

Commenced production of Face Veneer in Myanmar

2015

Demerger of Decorative Business comprising of Laminates and allied products into Greenlam

2017

Face Veneer plant with capacity of 36,000 CBM has commenced production at Gabon in West Africa

2018

Production of Decorative Veneers starts at Bamanbore Plant

2019

Demerger of MDF Business and Pantnagar Plywood Unit into Greenpanel

2020

Introduction of E-Zero Range

2021

Foray into MDF segment

2023

Greenplys entry into future fittings with the launch of Greenply Samet Pvt. Ltd
